Chicken and Rice

Well seasoned diced chicken breast pan seared with onions and garlic, and simmered with rice, frozen vegetables in chicken broth. A perfect calorie packed meal for those with busy days.

Ingredients

  • Two diced chicken breasts
  • 2 tbsp. olive oil
  • 1 chopped onion
  • 3 minced garlic cloves
  • 3 tsp. smoked paprika
  • 1 1/2 tsp. cumin
  • salt and pepper
  • 3 cups WASHED white rice
  • 1 1/2 cup chicken broth
  • 1 cup of frozen mixed veggies

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  2. Add chicken, season with salt, pepper, paprika, and cumin for 5-7 minutes. Remove and set aside.
  3. Add onion in same pan can let it cook for 2-3 minutes.
  4. Add garlic and cook for another minute.
  5. Add the rice, broth, and frozen veggies. Stir well.
  6. Put the chicken in and simmer on low heat for 20-25 minutes. Liquid should be absorbed.
  7. Done
