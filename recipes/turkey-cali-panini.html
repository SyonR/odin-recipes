Turkey Cali Panini

A melty sandwich loaded with oven-roasted turkey, crisp bacon, spicy pepper jack cheese, and fresh veggies, all pressed into warm golden panini bread with smoky chipotle sauce.

Ingredients

  • Panini bread
  • 6 oven-roasted turkey deli slices
  • Fresh spinach
  • 2-3 tomato slices
  • Thinly sliced red or white onion
  • 2 slices pepper jack cheese
  • 3 strips cooked bacon
  • Chipotle sauce
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Preheat a panini press or grill pan over medium heat.
  2. Slice bread in half lengthwise.
  3. Spread chipotle sauce on both inner sides of the bread.
  4. Layer turkey, bacon, cheese, spinach, tomato, and onion evenly.
  5. Close the sandwich and put some oil on the top and bottom.
  6. Grill in the panini press or on the pan with a weight on top for 3â€“5 minutes per side, until golden and the cheese is melted.
  7. Done
