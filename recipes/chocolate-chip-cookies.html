Chocolate Chip Cookies

Homemade chocolate chip cookies cooked with brown sugars and brown butter. Everything brown!

Ingredients

  • 2 sticks of unsalted butter
  • 1 room temp egg
  • 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup cane sugar
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1 1/2 cups chocolate chips
  • 1 tsp. baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp. salt
  • Avocado oil

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a bowl melt the butter until it turns brown and the milk solids float up
  3. Add cane sugar, brown sugar. Stir well.
  4. Add egg and vanilla extract. Stir well.
  5. In another bowl, mix all purpose flour, baking soda, salt.
  6. Add the wet ingredites to the dry ingredies slowly, stir well.
  7. Add chocolate chips
  8. Get a metal tray, add baking paper, spray or brush with avocado oil
  9. Fold the dough into balls and then bake for 12 minutes until edges are golden
  10. Cool for 5 minutes
  11. Done
